Brownies

Typed in brownies on the interent and stumbled upon this recipe. Looks good!

Ingredients

  • butter
  • white sugar
  • eggs
  • vanilla extract
  • cocoa powder
  • all-purpose flour
  • salt
  • baking powder

Steps

  1. Melt the butter on the stove
  2. remove from heat and add the remaining wet ingredients
  3. Beat in the dry ingredients
  4. Spread the bater in the pan and bake
  5. Bake at 350 degrees for about 25 to 30 minutes
